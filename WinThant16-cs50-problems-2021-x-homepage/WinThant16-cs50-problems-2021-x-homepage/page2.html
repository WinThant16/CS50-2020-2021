Here, we will explore Piplup and its evolution.

Piplup is a light-blue, penguin-like Pokémon with a strong sense of self-pride. It lives along the seashore in colder climates. There are two white ovals on its chest and a small, light-blue marking resembles a crown above its beak. It has flipper-like arms and yellow feet with three toes each. Piplup has a strong sense of self-pride. Because of its pride, it does not usually accept food from humans and makes it difficult to bond with its caretakers. It has trouble walking on land but it is a skilled swimmer. It lives along the seashore in colder climates. It evolves into Prinplup which gradually evolves into Empoleon.

Prinplup is a blue, penguin-like Pokémon. Instead of arms, it has long wings that resemble flippers. These wings are strong enough to knock down trees. It also has a high sense of self-pride, which prevents it from forming groups of its kind. Prinplups can be foun in icy waters.

Empoleon is a large, navy-blue penguin-like Pokémon. It has a yellow beak that extends into three pointed horns that form a trident-shaped crest on its face. Large horns are a symbol of leadership and strength. On it's back are two raised gold bumps and a blue film that forms its tail. The edges of its wings can slice through ice floes. Empoleons will attack anyone that damages its pride.

Piplups and Prinplups have only water attributes, but Empoleons have Steel and Water as its type.

They are strong against Fire, Ground and Rock type Pokémons.

But they are weak against Grass and Electric type Pokémons.

Empoleons are also weak to Fighting type Pokémons.
